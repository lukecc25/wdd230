Scenario 1

Semiannual Board Meetinsg

The semiannual board meetings will be held on the first friday of January and June. This meeting will be open to the public and will go over any major events for that year. The citizens of Caldian will also have a chance to voice their issues or concerns with whatever has been happening as of late.

Scenario 2

Immigration Acceptance Vote

There will be a vote deciding on how we will welcome new immigrants to Caldian on June 11th for the safty and protection of Caldian citizens.

Scenario 3

Population Growth

Since the announcement of a new magic school opening up the population of Caldian has gone up 20% from last year.

Scenario 4

Emergency Information

Emergency information and procedures have been posted at every major intersection, and a panphlet detailing these procedures have been mailed out to every resident and will be updated/mailed again yearly.

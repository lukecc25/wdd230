Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

BYU Idaho

  • BYU-Idaho home page

The BYU-Idaho homepage shows visual hierarchy due to the fact that all pertinent information can be found when you click the main menu button at the top of the screen. This is because visual hierarchy simply means that the main or important information of the site is easy to reach.

PARC: Repetition

Amazon

  • Amazon home page

Amazon is a great example for PARC: Repetition because this means that something is repeated and seen on multiple pages. Amazon does this by constantly having the checkout cart available at the top right of the screen for whichever page you open or item you click on.

PARC: Alignment

Fox News

  • Fox News home page

Fox News is a great example for PARC: Alignment because on a small screen the news articles are aligned with captions under the pictures. When you go onto a bigger screen the news articles are lined up side by side in groups of three. This makes it easier to see all of the news articles that are on the home page.
